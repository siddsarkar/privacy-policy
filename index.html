ERP Auto Login - IITKGP - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our service ERP Auto Login - IITKGP (the "Browser Extension").

Collection of Information

The information that you provide to us directly are your ERP credentials which may include your ERP username, ERP password and ERP Security Question Answer.

Your ERP credentials are stored in your browser's local storage (enctypted or unencrypted) upon your choice. We do not have access to your ERP credentials in any way.

Use of Information

We use your ERP credentials to automatically login to ERP. We do not use your ERP credentials for any other purpose.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your ERP credentials with any third party. We do not sell your ERP credentials.

Third-Party Embeds

We do not use any third-party embeds in our Browser Extension.

Transfer of Information to the United States and Other Countries

We are based in India. We do not transfer your ERP credentials to the United States or any other country.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at siddsarkar@outlook.com

Your Choices

You are given choice to opt for PIN protection of your ERP credentials in the Browser Extension. If you choose to opt for PIN protection, you will be asked to enter a PIN every time you want to login to ERP.

You can choose not to provide us with your ERP credentials. In this case, you will have to manually login to ERP.

Your California Privacy Rights

We do not collect any information from you that would be subject to the California Consumer Privacy Act of 2018.

Accessing and Correcting Your Information

You can access and correct your ERP credentials by going to the extension's popup page and reset you ERP credentials.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your ERP credentials from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at siddsarkar@outlook.com
